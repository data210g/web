Required Texts for GBCC DATA210G

This is the primary text:

  • Discovering Knowledge in Data, Wiley, 2014 Main Text {DKD}

Working in data science means keeping up with advancements in the field (and it is a broad field). Here are some key supplemental texts we will be using as part of the course (all free):

  • An Introduction to Data Science, Stanton, Syracuse University (free/online, also avaiable for free via Apple iBooks) {IDS}
  • The Split-Apply-Combine Strategy for Data Analysis , Journal of Statistical Software April 2011, Volume 40, Issue 1. (free/online) {SAC}
  • Tidy Data, Journal of Statistical Software August 2014, Volume 59, Issue 10 (free/online) {TD}

These are some good sites to keep as reference:

  • Beginner’s Guide to R
  • Intro to R
  • R Cookbook
